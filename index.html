"Vision Straight Records (VSR) Presents..."

Vision Straight Discovery Showdown 2022

The Vision Straight Discovery (VSD) edition #1 2022 is a first of many shows to come, which aims at providing talented individuals a platform to showcase their musical talents, for a chance to become part of the next generation of superstars, and to be signed into the Vision Straight Records Brand, with loads of added advantages.

2022.05.15(SUN) ~ 22(SUN)

@ VS Concert Hall, Bamenda, Cameroon.

Main Program

Registeration

Registration is FREE. The earlier the better because we have a "first come, first served" policy

Auditions

Given that registration is free, we will organize a pre-auditioning date and communicate it to the registered candidates. This screening is to ensure that only the truly talented individuals appear before the esteemed VSB judges on the Show.

Showcase

candidateswho make it through the auditions will then advance to the show itself and perform before the judges. Fans from all around the world will be allowed to have a say in deciding the winner as well, by means of voting. The process will be progressive and in stages, and only the one who makes it to the last stage wins!

Awards

The winner gets a fresh record deal at vision straight records(VSR). Each participant gets rewarded for their efforts, according to how far you advance in the competition. Prizes and awards will be announced on the show.

Entertainement

There will be interludes of musical concert experiences where popular artists will perform live for the people.
